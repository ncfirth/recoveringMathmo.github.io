Antenna Team

Dr Nicholas Firth

Nick has a Ph.D. in computational chemistry and currently works as a postdoctoral research assistant in the progression of neurodegenerative diseases team at UCL. He spends his days modelling various measures of cognition and communicating these models to clinicians and, more recently, artists. He has expertise in both supervised and unsupervised learning, and excels at modelling particularly noisy life science datasets.
